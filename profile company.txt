1. Sanitary Valves & Fittings

Description

Sanitary Valves & Fittings are designed to provide hygienic, safe, and efficient fluid control for industries requiring the highest cleanliness standards. Manufactured from high-grade stainless steel, these components offer excellent corrosion resistance, smooth surface finishes, and reliable performance under demanding operating conditions.

Our product range includes butterfly valves, ball valves, check valves, diaphragm valves, unions, clamps, ferrules, elbows, tees, reducers, and other sanitary fittings suitable for food processing, dairy, beverage, pharmaceutical, cosmetic, and biotechnology industries.

2. Sensor & Switch

Description

Our Sensor & Switch products provide accurate detection, monitoring, and control solutions for industrial automation systems. Designed for high reliability and long service life, these devices help improve production efficiency while ensuring stable and precise machine operation.

The product range includes proximity sensors, photoelectric sensors, pressure switches, limit switches, temperature sensors, float switches, and various industrial sensing devices suitable for manufacturing, packaging, robotics, logistics, and process automation.

3. Pneumatic & Hydraulics

Description

Our Pneumatic & Hydraulic components are engineered to deliver efficient power transmission and motion control across a wide range of industrial applications. Built with durable materials and precision engineering, these products ensure consistent performance, reduced maintenance, and increased operational productivity.

We offer pneumatic cylinders, solenoid valves, air preparation units, hydraulic pumps, hydraulic valves, hoses, fittings, actuators, and related accessories for manufacturing plants, heavy machinery, automation systems, and industrial equipment.

4. PVC, PP, Rubber Material, etc.

Description

We supply a comprehensive range of industrial PVC, PP, rubber, and engineering materials designed to meet demanding applications involving chemical resistance, corrosion protection, and long-term durability. These materials are widely used in piping systems, fabrication, sealing, and industrial processing.

Available in various sizes, grades, and specifications, our products support industries such as water treatment, chemical processing, food manufacturing, construction, mining, and environmental engineering.

5. Timer, Counter & Solid State Relay

Description

Our Timer, Counter, and Solid State Relay solutions are designed to enhance industrial automation, process control, and electrical system reliability. These electronic control devices provide precise timing, counting, switching, and monitoring functions to improve production efficiency and operational accuracy.

Suitable for control panels, packaging machines, conveyor systems, manufacturing equipment, and automated production lines, our products are available in various configurations to meet different industrial requirements.

6. Laminated Solenoid

Description

Our Laminated Solenoids are designed to provide reliable electromagnetic actuation for industrial machinery, automation systems, and electrical control equipment. Manufactured with high-quality laminated cores, these solenoids offer fast response, stable operation, and long service life even in demanding industrial environments.

They are commonly used in solenoid valves, locking mechanisms, industrial automation, textile machinery, packaging equipment, printing machines, and various electromechanical control systems where dependable actuation is essential.
